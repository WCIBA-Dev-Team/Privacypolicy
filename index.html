WCIBA Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you voluntarily provide it:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number when submitting prayer requests or testimonies
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version to ensure app functionality
  • Location Information: Approximate location (only when you use the feature to find the nearest WSF location, and only used temporarily on your device)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and respond to prayer requests
  • To manage and display testimonies within the church community
  • To send service reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To identify and fix technical issues

Data Storage and Security

  • All personal information is stored securely in Firebase, a Google cloud service
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access
  • We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For storing prayer requests and testimonies
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For delivering notifications

These services may collect certain information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of notifications through app settings
  • Request removal of your prayer requests or testimonies

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
